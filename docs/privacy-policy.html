Privacy Policy for Grounds

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Grounds ("we," "our," "the app") is a coffee shop discovery and social app. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it's used, and who it's shared with.

Information We Collect

Location

Grounds asks for your location (While Using the App) to show coffee shops near you on the map, to calculate distance to them, and to confirm you're actually at a shop when you check in. Your coordinates are sent to the Google Places API and, as a fallback, Apple Maps/MapKit, solely to search for nearby coffee shops. We do not independently store or retain your location history — it is used only to make the live search request and to verify check-ins, and is not logged by us.

Sign in with Apple

Grounds uses Sign in with Apple for authentication. Depending on what you choose to share, we receive your name and/or a private relay or real email address, plus an anonymous Apple user identifier. This information is stored locally on your device only (in the app's local storage) to keep you signed in and personalize your profile. Grounds does not have a backend server, so this information is not transmitted to us or stored anywhere outside your device.

Purchases

Grounds Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment card details. Apple's handling of payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Content You Create

Your profile details are stored locally on your device. Check-ins — including any photo you choose to attach and the shop you checked into — are stored in Apple's CloudKit, in a public database shared across all Grounds users, so the community can see recent check-ins and check-in counts per shop. This requires you to be signed into iCloud on your device; if you aren't, check-ins still count locally but won't be shared with the community. Reviews you write are currently stored locally on your device only.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Third-Party Services

Grounds relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Places API — coffee shop data, ratings, photos, and reviews. Your search coordinates are sent to Google to perform this lookup. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Maps / MapKit — fallback shop search and turn-by-turn directions.
  • Apple Sign in with Apple, StoreKit & CloudKit — authentication, subscription purchases, and community check-in storage. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention & Your Choices

  • You can revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
  • You can revoke Grounds' access to your Apple ID at any time in Settings → [your name] → Sign in & Security → Sign in with Apple.
  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored data, including your profile and reviews. Check-ins already shared to the community (including any attached photo) remain in CloudKit's public database, since they're visible to other users; contact us (below) to request removal of specific check-ins.

Children's Privacy

Grounds is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if we introduce a backend for syncing content across devices). Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
