Privacy Policy for Grounds

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Grounds ("we," "our," "the app") is a coffee shop discovery and social app. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it's used, and who it's shared with.

Information We Collect

Location

Grounds asks for your location (While Using the App) to show coffee shops near you on the map and to calculate distance to them. Your coordinates are sent to Google Places API and, as a fallback, Apple Maps/MapKit, solely to search for nearby coffee shops. We do not operate a server, so we do not independently store or retain your location history — it is used only to make the live search request and is not logged by us.

Sign in with Apple

Grounds uses Sign in with Apple for authentication. Depending on what you choose to share, we receive your name and/or a private relay or real email address, plus an anonymous Apple user identifier. This information is stored locally on your device only (in the app's local storage) to keep you signed in and personalize your profile. Grounds does not have a backend server, so this information is not transmitted to us or stored anywhere outside your device.

Purchases

Grounds Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment card details. Apple's handling of payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Content You Create

Reviews, check-ins, photos, and profile details you add inside the app are currently stored locally on your device. This version of Grounds does not sync this content to a cloud backend or share it with other users' devices.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Third-Party Services

Grounds relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Places API — coffee shop data, ratings, and photos. Your search coordinates are sent to Google to perform this lookup. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Maps / MapKit — fallback shop search and turn-by-turn directions.
  • Apple Sign in with Apple & StoreKit — authentication and subscription purchases. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention & Your Choices

  • You can revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
  • You can revoke Grounds' access to your Apple ID at any time in Settings → [your name] → Sign in & Security → Sign in with Apple.
  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored data, including your profile, reviews, and check-in history.

Children's Privacy

Grounds is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if we introduce a backend for syncing content across devices). Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
