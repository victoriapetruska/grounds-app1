Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?
Grounds uses Sign in with Apple — tap "Continue with Apple" on the welcome screen and confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.

How do I cancel Grounds Pro?
Open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Grounds → Cancel Subscription. Subscriptions are managed entirely through your Apple ID, not inside Grounds.

Why can't I see coffee shops near me?
Make sure Location Services are enabled for Grounds in Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

How do I delete my data?
Grounds stores your profile and activity locally on your device. Deleting the app removes this data. You can also revoke Grounds' access to your Apple ID at Settings → [your name] → Sign in & Security → Sign in with Apple.
